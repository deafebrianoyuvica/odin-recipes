Indonesian's Fried Noodles

Description

Indonesian fried noodles, or "mie goreng," is a popular street food dish that showcases the vibrant flavors of Indonesian cuisine. This dish features stir-fried egg noodles tossed with a medley of vegetables, proteins like chicken, shrimp, or tofu, and seasoned with a savory blend of soy sauce, garlic, and chili. The result is a delightful combination of textures and tastes, with the noodles absorbing the rich flavors of the sauce while remaining slightly chewy. Mie goreng is often garnished with fresh herbs, fried shallots, and a squeeze of lime juice to enhance its flavor profile. It can be enjoyed as a standalone meal or served alongside other dishes, making it a versatile option for any occasion. Whether enjoyed at a bustling street vendor or prepared at home, Indonesian fried noodles are a beloved comfort food that captures the essence of Indonesia's culinary heritage.

Preparation Time 20 minutes
Cooking Time 15 minutes
Servings 2-4
Difficulty Medium
Origin Indonesia
Course Main Course

Ingredients

  • 200g egg noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 100g chicken, shrimp, or tofu (optional)
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
  • 1 teaspoon chili sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fried shallots and lime wedges for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the egg noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  3. Saut√© the minced garlic and sliced onion until fragrant.
  4. Add the chicken, shrimp, or tofu (if using) and cook until folly cooked.
  5. Stir in the carrot and cabbage, cooking until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Add the cooked noodles to the pan and toss to combine.
  7. Pour in the soy sauce, sweet soy sauce, and chili sauce (if using), mixing well to coat the noodles evenly.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with fried shallots and lime wedges.
